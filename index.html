Online Classes

Although breastfeeding is a natural process it doesn't always come naturally. With the common modern day practice of birthing interventions, breastfeeding can present quite challenging in the first few days.

Feel prepared with obstacles you may face. Arm yourself with an understanding of how lactation works and common hospital practice. These online classes are best taken prenatally but can be taken anytime along your breastfeeding journey.
